# Customer Onboarding & Marketing Engagement Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes customer signup data to understand onboarding trends, customer demographics, and marketing engagement behavior.  
The goal is to help businesses improve growth strategies and run more effective marketing campaigns.

---

## Aim of the Project
To analyze customer signup and profile data in order to:
- Understand when customers sign up
- Identify key age groups and countries
- Analyze marketing opt-in behavior

---

## Dataset
- File: customers.csv
- Total Records: 20,000

### Columns Used
- customer_id – Unique customer identifier  
- signup_date – Date when the customer signed up  
- age – Customer age  
- country – Customer country  
- marketing_opt_in – Whether the customer agreed to receive marketing messages  

---

## Tools & Technologies
- Python  
- Pandas & NumPy  
- Matplotlib & Seaborn  
- Google Colab  

---

## Analysis Performed
- Data cleaning and date conversion
- Daily and monthly customer signup analysis
- Customer segmentation by age group
- Country-wise customer distribution
- Marketing opt-in analysis across age groups

---

## Key Insights
- Customer signups show clear growth patterns over time
- Some age groups are more willing to opt into marketing
- A few countries contribute most of the customers
- Marketing opt-in data can be used for targeted campaigns

---

## Business Value
This analysis helps businesses:
- Improve customer onboarding experience
- Identify high-engagement customer groups
- Make data-driven marketing decisions
